About Us

Logistics & Mining Equipment Suppliers

Ludo Logistics(pty)ltd is a South African company which specialises in Logistics and mining equipment hire. Founded in 2013 the company is head quarted in the Limpopo province of South Africa. Ludo Logitics strives to offer an outstanding services to its clients and the importance of customer satisfaction can not be over emphasied in our company. Ludo Logistics has a fleet of state of the art horse and trailer trucks as well as mining equipment and machinery. The company has operations across South Africa and aims to serve the greater good of the country. The company has staff members who are driven to offer a quality service. It employs a great number of workers from the Limpopo and Mpumalanga province and seeks to employ more personnel. We at Ludo Logistics strive for excellence and would be delighted to serve you.
